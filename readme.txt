Display Google Buzz Timeline on your sidebar.

== Description ==

This plugin displays your Google Buzz's time line at your sidebar with your icon, descriptions.

Requires:
 - WP 2.8 or later
 - PHP 5.x or later

== Installation ==

Place all items in your /wp-content/plugins/ directory
and activate through the administration panel, and then go to the widget panel and
drag it to where you would like to have it!
